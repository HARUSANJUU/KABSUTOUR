| FACILITIES

DEANS OFFICE

The Dean's Office serves as the central administrative authority responsible for managing academic and operational affairs. Led by the dean, it oversees a range of responsibilities including academic programs, faculty matters, financial management, and institutional policies. The Dean's Office is the primary point of contact for students, faculty, and staff seeking assistance or guidance on matters related to the college's operations and policies.

REGISTRAR OFFICE

The Registrar's Office manages student records, course registrations, and academic policies. It handles enrollment, graduation requirements, transcript requests, and other administrative tasks related to academics.

RESEARCH AND EXTENSION SERVICE OFFICE

The Research and Extension Service Office coordinates research projects and community outreach programs. It connects academic expertise with real-world needs, fostering innovation and societal impact.

DAS & DET

The Department of Arts and Sciences covers a wide range of subjects like humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, and fine arts. It offers diverse educational programs.
The Department of Teacher Education prepares aspiring educators through rigorous coursework and hands-on teaching experiences, guided by experienced faculty.

STUDENT DEVELOPMENT SERVICES OFFICE

The Student Development Service Office supports student growth and well-being through a variety of programs and resources, including counseling, career guidance, and leadership development. It strives to foster a supportive and inclusive campus environment where students can thrive academically, personally, and professionally.
