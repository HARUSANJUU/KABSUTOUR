To apply for admission at CvSU Carmona, you generally need:

  1. 1. Filled Application Form: Complete the provided application form.
  2. 2. High School Report Card: Submit a copy of your high school grades.
  3. 3. Good Moral Character Certificate: Provide a certificate from your previous school indicating good behavior.
  4. 4. Birth Certificate: Submit a copy of your PSA (formerly NSO) Birth Certificate.
  5. 5. Entrance Exam Results: If applicable, include results from the entrance exam.
  6. 6. Application Fee: Pay the required application or entrance exam fee.
  7. 7. Check for Program-Specific Requirements: Some programs may have additional requirements, so check for any specific documents or exams related to your chosen field.

Make sure to confirm the latest requirements by checking the CvSU Carmona website or contacting the Admissions Office directly.
