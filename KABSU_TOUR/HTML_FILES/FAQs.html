Frequently Asked Questions

  1. 1. Fill out the Admission Application Form: https://apps.cvsu.edu.ph/admission/ and Download the Admission Application Form
  2. 2. Select your schedule of submission of requirements through the same link
  3. 3. Download and print the completed form. Attach your 1 x 1 photo (with white background and name tag) and affix your signature,
  4. 4. Complete the requirements and place all documents in a long white folder
  5. 5. Submit your requirements on-site at Cavite State University - Carmona Campus, Admission Office Main Building, 2nd Floor, Room 206A, on the day of your selected schedule
  6. 6. After submitting your complete requirements, you will receive an examination permit with your schedule. (For Transferee applicants, you will be given a Notice of Evaluation and Schedule for the evaluation)
  7. 7. Take the Entrance Examination on your Scheduled Date On-site
  8. 8. Then, WAIT for the Release of the Examination Results on May 17, 2024
  9. 9. Lastly, Wait for the Issuance of NOTICE OF ADMISSION (NOA) for the Admission Passers for enrollment.
